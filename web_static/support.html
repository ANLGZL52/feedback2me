Frequently Asked Questions

What is Feedback2Me?

Feedback2Me lets you create a personal feedback link. Share it with friends, followers, or colleagues, and they can submit anonymous feedback. AI analyzes all responses and creates a detailed report for you.

How do I create a feedback link?

Sign in with your Apple or Google account, then tap "Create new link" on the home screen. Your first link is a free demo (10 minutes, one response). After that, you can purchase premium link credits for 24-hour links with multiple responses.

How do I sign in?

Tap "Sign in" and choose either "Sign in with Apple" or "Sign in with Google". No password or email registration is needed — your Apple ID or Google account handles authentication securely.

What are premium links?

Premium links stay active for 24 hours and accept multiple feedback responses. You can get them by purchasing individual link credits from within the app.

Are there monthly or recurring fees?

No. Feedback2Me only sells one-time link credits. You pay when you need another 24-hour premium link. There is no recurring billing.

How do I request a refund?

Apple: Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow the instructions.

Google: Open Google Play Store, tap your profile, then open payments / order history and select the purchase to request a refund.

Is my feedback anonymous?

Yes. When you write feedback for someone, your name is optional. The link owner sees only the feedback text, mood rating, and optional name — not your account details.

How do I delete my account?

Send an email to support@feedbacktome.app from the email address associated with your account, requesting account deletion. We will process your request within 30 days and permanently delete all your data, including feedback links, reports, and profile information.

What data do you collect?

We collect your sign-in information (name, email), feedback text, and usage data needed to run the service. We do not sell your data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Close and reopen the app.
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store or Google Play.
  • If the issue persists, email us at support@feedbacktome.app with a description of the problem and your device model.
