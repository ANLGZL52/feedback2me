Get the App

Feedback2Me is available on iOS (iPhone & iPad) and Android. Download from the App Store or Google Play to create feedback links, collect responses, and view AI-powered reports.

How It Works

1. Sign in with Apple or Google.

2. Create a personal feedback link.

3. Share the link — anyone can write anonymous feedback.

4. AI analyzes all responses into a detailed report.
